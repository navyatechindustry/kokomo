The Curation

A Quiet Escape in Mumbai - Your Calm Corner

At Cafe Kokomo., we’re not just about good food — we’re about warm hugs in croissant form, cozy corners, and coffee conversations that linger.

"We believe in doing things slow and sweet — sourcing local where we can, growing a few greens of our own, and pouring love into every croissant, brew, and bake."

Behind Cafe Kokomo. is a small team with big hearts, passionate about creating a space where people can truly unwind, connect, and feel welcomed. We’re dreamers, bakers, coffee lovers, and hosts who genuinely love bringing people together over good food and warm hospitality.
